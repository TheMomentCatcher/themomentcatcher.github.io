Pieces of sound

The soundscape is not just sound, it is like a picture, it must transfer the listener into the recording environment, it must evoke emotions and memories.

Nowadays, it is increasingly difficult to find a place where the sound environment is clean and unpolluted by aircraft, motor vehicles and human presence.

These are some of my recordings that are very special to me, most of them were recorded in places where I went on vacation with my family or on a trip to the mountains.

I hope you like them!
